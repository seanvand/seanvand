Kobe Bryant Tribute Timeline

Kobe Bryant

The Black Mamba


1996: The high school basketball star skips college and goes straight to the NBA

1998: Kobe became the youngest player, 19 years old, to play in an All Star Game

2000: Kobe wins his first NBA Title alongside Shaq

2001 and 2002: Kobe wins two more titles with Shaq as they complete the 3-peat

2008: Kobe wins first Olymipc Gold Medal with Team USA

2009: Kobe wins 4th title and is named Finals MVP

2010: Kobe wins fifth and final NBA title and another Finals MVP

2012: Kobe wins another Olympic Gold Medal with Team USA

2014: Kobe surpasses Micheal Jordan as 3rd on the all-time scoring list

2016: Kobe retired, and went out in triumph with a 60 point performance

2017: Kobe gets both number 8 and 24 retired in the Staples Center
